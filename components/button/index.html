Buttons
The buttons component in our design system offers a versatile set of interactive elements tailored to facilitate user actions and navigation within our interfaces. With customizable styles for size, shape, and color, it adapts seamlessly to various contexts and design requirements. Whether it's a primary call-to-action, a secondary option, or a tertiary action, our buttons component provides consistency and clarity across different screens and devices.
